About Us

Hakma Brand Consulting is a boutique Brand, Marketing Communications and Customer Experience firm. We create tailor made integrated strategies for your brand, to develop a deeper connection with your target audience.

Attention is currency, and we help you own it. We work with entrepreneurs, professionals, and public figures who are ready to control the narrative, sharpen their presence, and stand out with authority online and offline.

Your work has earned its place. Our role is to make sure your presence does too, so you’re remembered for the right reasons.
